Tongliang An

Ph.D. in management, Professor, Ph.D. Supervisor, Chair of the Department of Economics, Part-time Independent Director of Nanjing Chixia Development Co. Ltd., and Anonymous Reviewer of the journal of Economic Studies. He obtained the Special Prize of the Excellent Teaching Achievement Award of Higher Education of Jiangsu Province and the Second Prize of the National Excellent Teaching Achievement Award in 2005, and was funded by the New Century Excellent Talents Support Program of the Ministry of Education in 2006. Since 2005, he has successively published 5 academic papers in the SSCI journals and the journal of Economic Studies.

Research Area

  1. Industrial Economics
  2. Enterprise Growth
  3. Securities Investment
