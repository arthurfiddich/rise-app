Bio

Reverend Clement Aapengnuo is a Catholic priest from northern Ghana, in residence at St. Charles Borromeo Catholic Church. Fr. Aapengnuo teaches Catholic peacemaking at Georgetown University, Washington DC while working on his doctorate at the School for Conflict Analysis and Resolution (ICAR), George Mason University, Arlington VA. After 12 years of pastoral ministry, administration and teaching social communications, Rev. Aapengnuo was appointed director of the Northern Ghana Peace project in Damongo in 2000. Rev. Aapengnuo is a conflict analyst and coach with a lot experience in conflict resolution intervention strategies for communities in conflict. He has also designed conflict sensitive interventions and projects for development agencies working in post conflict zones.
