Shuzo ABE

Professor

Academic Area(s):

Marketing

Links: Email

Research & Teaching Interests

“Let us find a better way to serve customers by analyzing untapped data.”

Bio

Special Assistant at Hitotsubashi University (1972-1974), Instructor at Nihon University (1974-1977), Assistant Professor at Nihon University (1977-1979), Assistant Professor at Yokohama National University (1979-1987), Professor at Yokohama National University (1987-2009), Professor at WASEDA Business School (2009-Present)

  • President of Japan Association for Consumer Studies (1995)
  • President of Japan Society of Marketing and Distribution (2000-2001)
  • Editor of Advances in Consumer Studies (Japanese: 2001-2005)
  • Director of Consumer Research Institute at WASEDA University ( 2009-President)

Academic Degrees

B.B.A., Commerce, Meiji University, 1963-1967
M.S., Commerce, Hitotsubashi University, 1967-1969
Ph.D., Commerce, Hitotsubashi University, 1969-1972

Selected Publications

  • Cho, Hyun-Chul and Abe, Shuzo. (2012). Is the use of two-tailed testing for directional research hypotheses tests legitimate? Journal of Business Research (forthcoming)
  • Cho, Hyun-Chul and Abe, Shuzo. (2011). Overreliance on statistical testing logic in the empirical testing of theories and hypotheses. Journal of Global Academy of Marketing Science, 21/45-54.

Courses Taught

Currently teaching in Japanese only.
